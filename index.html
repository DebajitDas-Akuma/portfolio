Who I Am

I'm Debajit Das — a 17-year-old science student and developer from Tripura, India. I think in systems, build with code, and explore the edges of what's technically possible.

My work sits at the intersection of software development, robotics, and AI. I'm not interested in building for the sake of it — I build things that solve real problems, with real users in mind.

Whether it's writing clean backend logic, designing intuitive interfaces, or wiring up a microcontroller — I approach every challenge with the same mindset: understand deeply, then build precisely.
